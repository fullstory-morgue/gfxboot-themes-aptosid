Diegimas -- ACPI uždraustas

Current hardware usually requires ACPI (Advanced Configuration and Power Interface) to control the interrupt handling. ACPI completely replaces the old APM system.

Select Diegimas -- ACPI uždraustas if you encounter problems during boot of the kernel. Known problems with machines that have problems with ACPI are:

  • branduolys kraunantis užlūžta
  • PCI plokštės tinkamai neaptiktos ar nepaleistos

You may also try the Programinės aparatinės įrangos patikrinimas boot option and look at the test results of the ACPI validation.
